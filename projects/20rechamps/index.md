Virtual CityScope Champs-Élysées is an interactive and immersive platform for exploring the future of Paris' most important street. CityScope Champs-Élysées is a tangible user interface for urban planning and decision-making. The project explores new plans and future interventions for the Champs-Élysées Avenue, including reduced traffic, improved walkability, cycling and pedestrian mobility, and improved access to cultural landmarks and amenities. After the exhibition closed during COVID-19, the project moved online.
