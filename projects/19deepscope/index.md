DeepScope is a platform for interactive, real-time, low-setup urban design visualization. It explores how machine-learned, generative visualization can support urban design practice. The project uses a deep convolutional generative adversarial network (DCGAN) and CityScope, a tangible user interface, to enable real-time prototyping and visualization of urban design processes.
