Ariel Noyman, PhD, is an urban scientist, architect, and urban designer whose work explores [Street Knowledge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EzzsXhp_lig&themeRefresh=1): how design and technology can democratize data-driven urban processes.

[His research](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=2QzGsBYAAAAJ&hl=en) bridges [urban modeling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ywCV0ekYzyo), real-time simulation, and participatory design. At MIT, Noyman developed [CityScope](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YD0IbhaaqEc), an urban human-computer interaction platform deployed in cities worldwide, and helped establish a global network of City Science living labs active in more than 12 cities, including [Hamburg](https://medium.com/mit-media-lab/shifting-priorities-finding-places-9ad3bdbe38b8), [Andorra](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bRNbap13I5w), [Shanghai](https://tjdi.tongji.edu.cn/about.do?ID=75&lang=_en), [Helsinki](https://www.aalto.fi/en/group-x/city-science-lab-at-aalto-2018-19-a-collaboration-with-mit-media-lab), Detroit, and the [Negev](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Hgm2MQnYjcw).

Noyman's work has received international recognition, including a European Union UrbanAct Award, first place in Rebuild by Design, and first prize in the Museum of Tolerance design competition. His work has been featured by [The Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com/cities/2014/dec/18/lego-toys-urban-planning-tool-architects-mit), 60 Minutes, [The New York Times](https://www.nytimes.com/2018/04/03/business/smart-city.html), [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2018-08-03/how-ar-can-transform-the-way-cities-work), [Harvard Business Review](https://hbr.org/2025/08/how-ai-can-help-tackle-collective-decision-making?tpcc=orgsocial_edit), and MIT News, and has been shown in exhibitions, conferences, and summits worldwide.

Today, Noyman is a [Research Scientist](https://www.media.mit.edu/people/noyman/overview/) at the MIT City Science Center. He has taught urban science, urban design, and architecture studios at MIT, Cornell Tech NYC, Northeastern University, CUNY, and [Bezalel Academy](https://hansen.co.il/event/%D7%A2%D7%99%D7%A8%D7%95%D7%A0%D7%99%D7%95%D7%AA-%D7%91%D7%A2%D7%99%D7%93%D7%9F-%D7%94%D7%A8%D7%9B%D7%91-%D7%94%D7%90%D7%95%D7%98%D7%95%D7%A0%D7%95%D7%9E%D7%99/). Before MIT, he practiced architecture, urban design, and city planning for over a decade in the US and EMEA. Noyman holds a PhD and SM from MIT and a B.Arch, cum laude, from Bezalel Academy.
